Kranji Express:

Take the Kranji Express bus from Kranji MRT Station which operates daily from 8.30 am to 5.45 pm.From Kranji Gate, it is an approximately 1 km walk along Neo Tiew Lane 2 to the observation tower.

Parking:

Alternatively, a car park with 20 parking lots, 10 motorcycle lots and 30 bicycle lots is available at Kranji Gate.
